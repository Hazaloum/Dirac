Search results:
{search_results}

- Include ATC code for every molecule
- Patent status must be one of: Expired | Expiring within 1 year | Protected

- Patent-protected molecules score maximum 6 unless an active licensing pathway exists
- Score must reflect market saturation, not just disease prevalence
- Rationale must reference at least two scoring factors, not just disease burden
